This file describes a possible issue with certain audio devices and steps to correct the
problem.

Some users of Ensoniq and Creative Labs PCI audio devices, when combined with other 
combinations of hardware in the PC, may experience random changes in the current system 
date setting.  This problem may occur with any of the following audio devices:

- Ensoniq AudioPCI
- Creative Labs Sound Blaster PCI64
- Creative Labs Sound Blaster PCI128

You can verify which device you have by checking in Device Manager located in the Control
Panel's System applet.

If you are experiencing this problem, it can be corrected with newer drivers from
one of the following web sites:
 
- http://www.ensoniq.com
- http://www.soundblaster.com

Newer drivers are generally located in either a Support or Download area of a web site.

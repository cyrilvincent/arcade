Multi-computers Front-End for computer's emulators.

- Able to show you CpcEmu and specifics disk parameters.
- Auto display of Ams Dos catalog and Cp/m 2.2 statut.
- Auto display CP/m 68000 [MP/M], CP/m 86 and CP/m v.3 for MSX (Raw formats).
- Auto display Pc, Msx Dos and generics catalogs (Fat12 formats). 
- Auto display of Apple PRODOS catalog.
- Auto display of Linux image disks (partials informations).
- Auto display of Dos 2.xx catalogs for Atari/Atari XL.
- Auto display of Atari ST [ST DOS] catalog.
- Auto display of Amiga Dos [ADF files] catalog.
- Auto display of Ti99a or Geneve V9t9 and PC99 catalogs [IMG;DSK].
- Auto display of TRS DOS ,New DOS and OS9 (DMK or JVC format)[DSK files](for TRS/80 "CoCo series" or Dragon 16/32) catalogs.
- Auto display of Maxi Dos [SAD files] (for Sam Coupe) catalogs.
- Auto display of Oric1, Oric Atmos and Pravetz 8d [SedOric DOS ... MFM format] catalog.
- Auto display of BBC model B/B+ [BBC OS: IMG, BBC & SSD format] catalog.
- Auto display of Thomson models [fd format] catalog.
- Auto display of Altair models [dsk format] catalog.
- Auto display of X68000 models [xdf format] catalog.
- Auto display of ZX81 files and tapes.
- Auto display of ZX81 disk images files on Larken drives controllers.
- Auto display of consoles.
- Launch disks images formats to your favorite emulator.
  (limitation of emulators config, can be changed in codes)
- Support Zip format, and can auto-reZip all changed image disk structure.  
- SNA structures can be scanned and display.  
- Able Zip-tags with your ascii arts.
- Visual Display of incompatible disk image with CpcEmu Dsk format. 
- Display somme PC, Msx, Ti99 and Coco image disk, to move it in the good directory.  
- Now enable a graphic disk mapping and allocations errors on CPC disks.
- Several convertions filters.
- Binary tools to trim, patch or convert current  disks images formats . (cut headers or add a standard boot sector)

- Advenced initialize function for CPCemu format (1 side only-> tag 0).
  (this format don't support 2 sides disks check your emulator's documentation.)
- Multi-languages support (just change Language key in DskCenter.ini or GUI)

  English (default): Language=
  French           : Language=FR
  

Scan feature may be changed to display other computer's disks devices!
